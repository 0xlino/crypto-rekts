**Quick Summary**

The BEAR token recently experienced a rug pull incident, leading to a significant loss of approximately $202.8K USD in 109.5 Ethereum (WETH) tokens.

  


 **Details of the Exploit**

The project team behind the BEAR token executed a rug pull by performing liquidity removal from the Uniswap V2 pool. As a result, they successfully withdrew and transferred a total of 109.5  WETH, equivalent to $202.8K  USD, distributing the ill-gotten funds across four different wallets.

  


This sudden liquidity removal and token transfer event caught the community off guard, causing substantial financial losses to investors and holders of BEAR token.
